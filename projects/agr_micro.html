Executive Summary

MBL is spearheading the development of a supercomputing-based integrated system for the systematic management of agri-microbiome data. Supported by the Rural Development Administration (RDA) for a four-year period from May 2025 to December 2028, this project focuses on optimizing Shotgun Metagenomics pipelines and establishing an automated analysis platform to standardize data management protocols across the agricultural, livestock, and food sectors.

01. Pipeline Standards

Defining optimal metadata protocols and analysis cut-lines for high-precision shotgun metagenomics.

02. Data Integration

Building a multi-omics database linked with diverse environmental variables and crop health indicators.

03. UI/UX Automation

Designing a web-based automated platform for seamless bioinformatic processing and advanced visualization.
