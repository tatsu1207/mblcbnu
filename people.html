Principal Investigator

Dr. Tatsuya Unno

Professor

tatsu@cbnu.ac.kr

Dr. Unno directs the Microbiome Lab at Chungbuk National University. His research spans environmental microbiology, computational metagenomics, host-microbe interactions, and **antibiotic resistant bacteria**. He received his Ph.D. from **Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology [GIST]**.

"Our goal is to translate complex genomic data into actionable knowledge for public health and environmental sustainability."
