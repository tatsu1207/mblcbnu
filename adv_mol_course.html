Course Plan: Advanced Molecular Biology (고급분사생물학)

Week Topic Activity / Focus Reference
1 Orientation Course overview, evaluation criteria, and gene assignments. Orientation
2 The Central Dogma DNA Replication (Helicase, Ligase), Transcription (Sigma factors), Translation, and Splicing mechanisms. The Central Dogma
3 Genomes & Regulation Bacterial genome structure (Pan-genome), Operons (lac/trp), and RNA thermometers. Genomes & Regulation
4 Genetics & Evolution Horizontal Gene Transfer (Transformation, Conjugation, Transduction, Vesiduction) and Mutations. Genetics & Evolution
5 PCR Technologies Standard PCR principles, Gradient PCR optimization, and Real-time PCR quantification methods. PCR Technologies
6 DNA Sequencing Sanger sequencing (Chain termination) vs. NGS (Illumina flowcell chemistry & Nanopore). DNA Sequencing
7 Mid-course Recap Review of Weeks 1–6 (Central Dogma, Regulation, PCR, Sequencing) and Q&A session. -
8 Mid-term Exam Comprehensive theoretical assessment covering Weeks 1–6. -
9 Gene Cloning Restriction enzymes, Ligation, Transformation techniques (Heat shock/Electroporation), and Blue-White screening. Gene Cloning
10 Genetic Engineering Site-directed mutagenesis, Homologous recombination, and CRISPR/Cas9 systems. Genetic Engineering
11 Bioinformatics BLAST algorithms (blastn/p/x), Database search, and Frame shift analysis. Bioinformatics
12 Transcriptomics RNA-seq workflow: Library preparation, Ribo-zero, Read mapping, and DEG analysis. Transcriptomics
13 Metagenomics Gut microbiome analysis, 16S rRNA vs Shotgun sequencing, and Host-Microbe interaction case studies. Metagenomics
14 Makeup Class I Buffer for missed content or Recapping of Genetic Engineering & Omics (Weeks 9–13). -
15 Makeup Class II Final review session and laboratory troubleshooting / Research Design Workshop. -
16 Final Exam Final cumulative assessment covering all topics. -
