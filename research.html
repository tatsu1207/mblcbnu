Development of a WGS-based Analysis Platform for ARB Assessment
항생제 내성균 WGS 분석 Tool 개발

(2026.01 - 2027.12)

Project Description: This project aims to establish a comprehensive Whole-Genome Sequencing (WGS)-based integrated analysis platform for systematically managing antibiotic-resistant bacteria (ARB) of livestock origin. The research focuses on developing standardized analysis protocols, machine learning-based prediction models for resistance and mobility, and an end-to-end user interface for national-level antimicrobial resistance (AMR) surveillance and risk evaluation.

  • Establishment of standardized WGS SOPs using Illumina and Oxford Nanopore (ONT) Hybrid sequencing to optimize the reconstruction of complex plasmids and mobile genetic elements.
  • Development of Explainable AI (XGBoost) models for predicting antibiotic susceptibility (AST) phenotypes and plasmid transferability based on functional genomic features.
  • Construction of an integrated SQL-based database and UI for researchers to perform automated analysis, risk scoring, and interactive visualization of transmission routes.

Development of a Public Service for the Agri-Bio Microbiome Database
농생명 마이크로바이옴 데이터베이스 공공 서비스 개발

(2025.06 - 2028.12)

Project Description: This project aims to provide integrated services through the development of data standardization, a supercomputing-based analysis system, data bank construction, and quantitative analysis technology for the systematic management of the agricultural and life science microbiome.

  • Development of web-based portal for microbiome data access.
  • Analysis of antibiotic resistance genes (ARGs) in diverse environments.
  • Metagenomic assembly and functional annotation of unculturable bacteria.
