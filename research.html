The Microbiome Lab employs a multidisciplinary approach, bridging microbiology, bioinformatics, and environmental science to tackle urgent challenges related to bacterial ecology, disease, and resistance.

Computational Metagenomics & Genomics

농생명 마이크로바이옴 데이터베이스 공공 서비스 개발

(2025.06 - 2028.12)

Project Description: This project aims to provide integrated services through the development of data standardization, a supercomputing-based analysis system, data bank construction, and quantitative analysis technology for the systematic management of the agricultural and life science microbiome.

  • Development of web-based portal for microbiome data access.
  • Analysis of antibiotic resistance genes (ARGs) in diverse environments.
  • Metagenomic assembly and functional annotation of unculturable bacteria.
