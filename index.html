Core Research Areas

Host-Pathogen Interactions

Investigating the molecular mechanisms used by bacteria to evade the host immune system, focusing on signaling pathways and virulence factors.

RNA-Seq and Genomics

Applying next-generation sequencing and bioinformatics to analyze transcriptomes, identify biomarkers, and predict antibiotic resistance profiles.

Biofilm Dynamics

Study of bacterial community structures (biofilms) and discovery of novel compounds or enzymes capable of disrupting these protective layers.
