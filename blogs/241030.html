MSK 2024 at Jeju ICC

Published on October 30, 2024

I gave a talk at the MSK meeting on investigating the dissemination of antibiotic-resistant bacteria through whole genome sequencing. I was the final speaker before the award lecture, but thankfully, some participants stayed until the end of the meeting.

This talk was originally prepared in Korean, but an hour before my presentation, Prof. Sadowsky mentioned he would attend only if I presented in English. So, although I wasn't fully prepared, I presented in English……
