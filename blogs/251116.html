MBL The 1st Homecoming Day in Jeju

We are thrilled to share that we held our very first Homecoming Day in the beautiful setting of Jeju Island. This special event brought together alumni and current members of the lab for a meaningful time of connection and professional exchange.

The program was packed with both intellectual and physical activities. We held a small symposium where members shared their current individual research topics.

In addition to the academic sessions, we also participated in a marathon together, fostering teamwork and health outside of the lab environment. It was a wonderful opportunity to blend cutting-edge research discussion with fun, shared physical activity, creating lasting memories for all attendees.
