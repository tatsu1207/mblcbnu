Home coming day in Jeju

Published on November 16, 2025

Alumni and current lab members gathered in Jeju for a wonderful homecoming event. The weekend was filled with exciting activities, including a local marathon and a small symposium where past and present members shared their research and experiences.

It was a fantastic opportunity to reconnect, share knowledge, and strengthen the bonds of our community. The marathon was a great challenge, and the symposium sparked many new ideas and potential collaborations.

Event Highlights:

  • Participation in the Jeju International Marathon.
  • Alumni research presentations and updates.
  • Networking dinner and social gathering.
  • Strategic planning session for the upcoming year.

We extend our heartfelt thanks to all who participated and made this event a memorable success. We look forward to seeing everyone again next year!
