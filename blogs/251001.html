Summer Workshop: Bioinformatics

Published on October 01, 2025

This year's summer workshop focused on advanced bioinformatics techniques, including metagenomic analysis and machine learning applications in microbiology. Participants gained hands-on experience with various tools and platforms.

Dr. Unno presented on the latest developments in ARB surveillance using WGS data, highlighting the lab's ongoing projects.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Metagenomic data processing and annotation
  • Phylogenetic analysis of microbial communities
  • Application of AI/ML in predicting antibiotic resistance
  • Interactive data visualization

The workshop concluded with a poster session where participants presented their preliminary research findings, fostering collaborative discussions.
