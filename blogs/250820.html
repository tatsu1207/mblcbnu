Lab Retreat: Team Building

Published on August 20, 2025

The Microbiome Lab recently enjoyed a productive and fun-filled retreat focused on team building and strategic planning for upcoming projects. Activities included outdoor challenges, collaborative problem-solving sessions, and a delicious barbecue.

These retreats are vital for fostering a strong team spirit and encouraging innovative thinking outside the traditional lab environment.

Retreat Activities:

  • Hiking and nature exploration
  • Brainstorming sessions for new research directions
  • Group culinary challenge
  • Evening campfire and discussions

Everyone returned refreshed and motivated, ready to tackle new scientific challenges with renewed energy and cohesion.
