New Publication: Microbial Ecology

Published on September 15, 2025

Our latest research article, "Dynamics of Microbial Communities in Freshwater Ecosystems," has been published in the Journal of Microbial Ecology. This study investigates the seasonal variations and influencing factors of microbial diversity in local river systems.

The findings provide crucial insights into the health of aquatic environments and the role of microorganisms in nutrient cycling and pollutant degradation.

Highlights of the Study:

  • Identified key microbial species indicative of water quality.
  • Analyzed the impact of agricultural runoff on microbial composition.
  • Applied next-generation sequencing techniques for comprehensive community profiling.

We believe this work will contribute to better environmental management strategies and conservation efforts.
