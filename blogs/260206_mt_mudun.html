Lab Retreat with Dr. Song's Lab at Mudeungsan Mountain

February 6, 2026

On a wonderfully warm day, our lab had the pleasure of a joint retreat with Dr. Song's Lab from Chosun University. We embarked on a trip to the beautiful Mudeungsan Mountain in Gwangju, South Korea.

The day was filled with engaging research discussions, allowing us to share insights and explore new ideas in a relaxed environment. Beyond the academic exchange, we also enjoyed a fantastic lab retreat together, strengthening our camaraderie and creating memorable experiences.

It was a truly valuable experience, fostering collaboration and team spirit between both labs.
